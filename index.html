How It Works

Online in
four steps

STEP 01

Pick a symbol

Choose from 9 unique symbols. Your opponent gets assigned a different one automatically — no conflicts.

STEP 02

Create a room

Enter your nickname and hit Create. A 4-digit room code generates instantly with a shareable invite link.

STEP 03

Invite & join

Send the link. Your opponent opens it, enters their name, clicks Join. Both players connect in real time.

STEP 04

Play & chat

Moves sync instantly across devices. Use the built-in chat to taunt, congratulate, or call a rematch.
